About

This is a Todo List app.

To add an item, fill the "Add Todo..." field and click on Add.

To edit an item, click on the title, then press "Enter" or "Esc" to cancel. Ps: If you change the title and focus on another item, it will save the title automaticaly.

To complete the item, just click on the checkbox on the left.

To remove the item, jsut click on the "X" button on the rigth.
